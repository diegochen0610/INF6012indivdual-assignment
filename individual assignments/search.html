MEET THE WEBMASTER

My name is Paul and I am an amateur astronomer just like you! I am an experienced deep sky observer and I have logged over 1000 deep sky objects in over 40 years of observing. My main telescope is a Meade LX200 GPS 10-inch, but I also own a 12-inch GSO Dobsonian, a 10-inch SkyWatcher Dobsonian, a 5-inch SkyWatcher reflector, a Celestron NexStar 114GT, and an Orion 80ED.
